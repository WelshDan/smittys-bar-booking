Live Events

Come and enjoy our live events in the Main Bar

Pub Quiz

Thursday nights

Join The Three Amigos as they host their quiz every Thursday night - 19.00 start.

  • Don't forget to book your table!
  • The quiz is free.
  • A variety of topics and difficulty level.

The Three Amigos have hosted the quiz since the bar opened in 2005. The running time is normally between 19.00-21.30. Everything needed is provided by the quizmasters, so just turn up and enjoy yourself.

Live Music

Wednesdays, Fridays and Saturdays

We have a range of live artists performing. Some to dance to and some to sit and enjoy.

  • Wednesday night is troubadour night. More low-key but still lots of fun!
  • Friday nights are reserved for live music to kick the week off with some energy.
  • Saturday nights are for the headline artist of the week.

Live acts normally play between 20:00 and 23:00. Be aware of your table position when booking. Some tables are closer to the stage which can be noisy! That can be preferrable for some people though.
The headline act changes from week to week. It could be a new young act, a cover band, a soul singer or, occasionally a well-known act. Previous live acts included Zara Larsson, Tom Jones, Jules Holland and the Manic Street Preachers.

Stand Up

Monday nights

"I Don't Like Mondays" sang the Boomtown Rats in 1979, well, Mondays don't have to be so bad! If you like a laugh then come along!

  • Acts normally start at 21.00.
  • Bring a sense of humour!
  • Jokes are more fun with friends.

We have hosted Stand Up comedians since 2012 and we think it is a great start to the week. We are always open to booking new comedians to give them a chance. We have even booked some famous from around the world. Some huge successes were Sean Locke, √ñzz Nujen and Ali Wong.
